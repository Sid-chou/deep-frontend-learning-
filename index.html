To my cutest Pyaare and sweetest

Happy Valentine's Day to my dearest, closest, my pyaare cutest lovely sweetest human being. Ever since you allowed me to enter in your life, the privilege to talk every single day is nothing more than a blessing to me.

I want to appreciate your real beauty, not from the outside but from the inside. Anyone can say you are beautiful, which no doubt you are, but the things I have experienced when I talk to you makes me fond of you.
You walk into a room, and people notice without even trying—it's like your presence changes the whole atmosphere, the energy shifts just because you showed up.
Your beauty which is the most easy thing to point out, but when I see in you it takes attention to point out, takes knowing you, understanding you. Even after all these years, it makes it unforgettable.
